Two Factor Authentication

Facial Recognition and Rhythm Recognition 2FA schemes


Facial Recognition

When using this scheme, you would first enter a password and then your identity would be confirmed using
facial recognition software and your devices camera.


Rhythm Recognition:

When using this scheme, you would first enter a password and then your identity would be confirmed by
rhythmically tapping your device or rhythmically clicking your mouse in a pattern you had previously created.
